§23

Quality Control

Customers care most about batch stability, traceability, and closed-loop handling of exceptions.

Raw Material Control

PP pellets are inspected batch by batch before storage for melt index, ash content, and moisture; non-conforming raw materials are rejected.

Production Process Control

Temperature, pressure, speed, and tension are monitored online in real time, with automatic alarms for key parameters.

Key Parameter Testing

Basis weight, thickness, tensile strength, and elongation are sampled regularly to ensure a stable process window.

Finished Product Inspection

Each finished roll undergoes visual inspection and full physical performance testing before off-line, with data archived.

Sample Retention

Samples from each batch are retained for at least 12 months for retesting and traceability.

Batch Traceability

Full-chain barcode management from raw-material batch to finished-roll number, enabling forward/backward traceability at any node.

Shipment Confirmation

Batch, specification, and quantity are verified before shipment, with QC reports attached.
